Our Background

Baitul Ehsan Al-Khairi Organization, commonly known as Baitul Ehsan Al-Khairi, is a charitable organization that commenced its operations in 2000 and was officially registered in 2012. We focus on supporting the Asnaf groups, including orphans, the poor, new converts (Muallaf), and single mothers. With over two decades of history in charitable work, the organization has been managed by various parties, inspired by the early initiatives of the family and companions of Prophet Muhammad SAW, expanding its activities to include food distribution throughout Kuala Lumpur.

Our Vision

Our Mission

Asatizah & Teachers

Led by experienced educators, our Asatizah focus on nurturing the love for the Al-Quran and the Sunnah. We strive to shape the students' character (Akhlaq) to ensure they grow up as responsible Muslims who contribute to the Ummah.
