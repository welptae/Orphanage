Current Needs of Baitul Ehsan Al-Khairi

We are currently focusing on the following 5 key areas:

Basic Needs & Operational Fund

Financial support covers daily expenses including food, clothing, and learning materials, as well as utility payments and maintenance.

Volunteer Teachers (Life Skills)

We are looking for volunteers to teach essential life skills such as cooking, sewing, and carpentry to build self-confidence and future readiness.

Volunteer Teachers (Academic)

We need volunteers to assist students in Mathematics, Science, Malay, and English. Your support is crucial to improving their academic performance.

Sports & Co-Curricular

Volunteer coaches are needed for football, futsal, and archery. These activities foster discipline, teamwork, and students' overall health.

Construction Fund (Hulu Langat)

We are establishing a new Maahad in Hulu Langat to accommodate more students. Funds are needed for construction costs, infrastructure, and basic facilities.
