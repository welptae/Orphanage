Giving Hope to the Future

Every child deserves a safe home, a chance to learn, and the opportunity to dream. Together, we can build that future.

Baitul Ehsan Al-Khairi is a shelter established to assist orphans, the underprivileged, the homeless, and new converts. We also provide support to children facing difficulties in accessing education, including refugees and those with documentation issues.

Our primary focus at Baitul Ehsan is education. We utilize a comprehensive homeschooling system that integrates academic learning with Quranic memorization (Hafazan) and Islamic studies. This holistic approach ensures that our children not only memorize the Quran but are also fully prepared to face the challenges of the future.

As the number of children under our care has grown, the home has expanded to accommodate more residents. Thanks to the unwavering support of our neighbors and the community, Baitul Ehsan has successfully secured a larger and more comfortable facility to ensure the safety and well-being of every child.
